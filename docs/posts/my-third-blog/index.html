My Third Reflection-Blog

April 3, 2026

Introduction

Welcome back to my third reflection blog. In this blog I will talk about my experiences during the third unit of the course- Information Science. The third unit was about Reflecting on failure. To reflect on failure we did some group projects where we dealt with some case studies and chapters from one very good book by Emy Edmondson on failure “The right kind of wrong: the science of failing well”. Actually the group project was that we had to go through the case study articles and chapters from the book and create a wiki page and then Individual Viva in front of the class. It was really interesting. I will tell you about everything in my blog ahead. So stay tuned and I will take you all through my journey.

First Chapter: Group Division

It was the very first day of the class after mid semester break when it all started. It was an evening class. Our class began with playing one game. The whole class was divided into two groups; group 0 and group 1. I was in group 1. Then the 1st group was told to go outside for a 5 minute break. After 5 minutes we were called in and group 2 was sent out. As we got in, anyone from the group was asked to volunteer to spin for the lucky number for the group. I took the opportunity. We got 87 as the lucky number. The number was big. wasn’t it?. Then the group was told to fill up one survey form. After the form was filled, group 0 was called in and the comparison of the difference in choices between the two groups were made. The instructor told the class the reason behind the differences in choices. The game was really interesting. Then at the end of the class the whole class was divided into some groups(groups of 4-5 members). Then she introduced the group project on failure and gave the instructions. By the time all this was done, it was already 8:30 pm so after this we went for dinner.

Last Chapter: Team work and Viva

There were five members in our group. Our case study was “Google Flu Trend” and Prologue and chapter 1 from the book ‘Right Kind of Wrong’. The Whole project was divided into 5 part. Some had the responsibility to do prologue, some chapter 1, some reflection, some to gather resources, and some case study. I had the responsibility to work on the overview of case study. I was provided the resources- many articles on Google flu Trend. I went through all of them and wrote down the overview of the case study in wiki style. All our group members did their work responsibily. They were all cooperative to each other, we worked together like brothers. After the task was finished, we all sat together and explained each other what we had done so far. This was also the preparation for Viva day.

On 1st april, wednesday, it was our Viva day, the end day of this project. We went for it all prepared. So the class began and one person from each group was randomly called out to present the project in front of the class. You see, this was the kind of Viva taken. Our group’s turn came at 4th number. One person from our group was called out and was asked to present the work, not me. He went ahead with full confidence and did his job. And this way we concluded our project work.

Reflection

Throughout the project work I learnt some very important lessons. Team work is very important. Whenever I think of team work this one quote strikes in my mind- “If you want to go fast go alone, but If you want to go far to together”. This quote is very real. Anyone who dream to achive big in life must learn to work together with other people because this is the one skill that can help you achieve that. Also I learnt other things as well like how to create a Wiki, how to do research, how to appriciate failure and also learnt that ‘failing is good when it is of right kind’.
