DIKW Model

Another example- The DIKW model explains the relationship between Data, Information, Knowledge, and Wisdom.

Age Vs Literacy Rate Graph(Bangalore district)

Key Observations:

  • Childhood Increase: Literacy rates rise sharply from age 7 to 9 as children progress through early schooling.

  • Peak Literacy: The literacy rate peaks and remains consistently high (above 95%) for the age groups between 11 and 19 years.

  • Gradual Decline: Starting from the 20-24 age group, there is a steady decline in the literacy rate. This trend is common in historical demographic data, reflecting higher educational access for younger generations compared to older ones.

  • Senior Population: The literacy rate is lowest in the 80+ age category, at approximately 65.8%.

Note: The ‘0-6’ age group was excluded from the graph as census data typically records a 0% literacy rate for this group, considering them pre-literate.
