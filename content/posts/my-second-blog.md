## Lab Activities
I believe that learning comes more from doing things practically than to just knowing about them theoretically. So we had lab activities where we used to apply the concepts learned to test and do things practically. During our First Unit of the course, we did some interesting things with **wiki and markdown**. Our group was given a chapter(*The talking Drum*) and prologue from the book *"The Information"* by ***'James Glieck'*** and we had to summarise the chapter using the concepts of wiki. The summarise work we did was on ***zettlr***. Evertime we did something during lab activites, we did that in groups. And from here comes the team work learning. In my past life, I always prefered to work alone. But through all the activities we did in groups during lab work, I learned how important the team work is. Its importance reminds me of one very renowned **African Proverb-** ***" If You want to go fast go alone, but If you want to go far go together".*** I would say this is a great skill that everyone must develop who wants to do something big in life(go far). Then we started doing something on which I am working now and I believe I will continue doing in future as well. We built a ***Hugo server*** and conected it to our **Github account**. This task took a lot of time. It was a lot that many people struggle to complete it till the end of Second Unit.
